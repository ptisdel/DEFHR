At Days End Farm Horse Rescue, nothing is better than watching a powerful bond form between horse and human. That’s what happened when Chris Covert met Elliot. Always interested in owning a horse, Chris decided she and her family were ready to make the commitment. DEFHR introduced her to Elliot, a two-year-old bay who proved to be the perfect match for her.
